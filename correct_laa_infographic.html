Total Revenue Mix: Where The Value Is

Our total revenue of $691,639 over the last 365 days clearly shows that members find the most value in our engagement opportunities, not just the base membership.

The Path Forward: A 3-Point Strategy

To build a sustainable future, we must shift our focus from serving everyone equally to serving our most engaged members exceptionally. This requires a strategic change in our operations.

1. Restructure Dues

💰

Align our pricing with the Texas market average to reflect our true value. This includes a new base plus per-unit fee for owner-properties and an updated rate for supplier partners.

Projected: +$22,000 in dues, even after accounting for potential loss of inactive members.

2. Implement Tiers

📊

Introduce a tiered service model to focus our resources where they generate the most impact. This protects our core revenue and minimizes time spent on unengaged members.

  • CORE (53): VIP treatment & personal management.
  • ACTIVE (117): Standard-plus service with growth incentives.
  • INACTIVE (674): Minimal, self-service model.

3. Rebalance Staff Time

⏰

Shift from a model where 65% of staff time serves the least engaged 80% of members. Free up our team to focus on high-value, revenue-generating activities and personalized CORE member service.

Goal: Reduce administrative burden by 50% to reinvest time in growth and retention.

The Bottom Line: A Stronger Future

We must stop trying to be everything to everyone and start being essential to the members who drive our success. This strategy builds a more resilient and prosperous LAA.
